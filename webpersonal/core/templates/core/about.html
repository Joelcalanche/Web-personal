Joel Calanche

Hello, my name is Joel Calanche. I am fundamentally Christian. I am Venezuelan. With degree in electrical engineer, graduated in 2019 from the National Experimental Polytechnic University "Antonio Jose de Sucre" (UNEXPO), I really like math, engineering sciences and now a computer science, during my career I excelled in subjects such as calculus, physics, electrical circuits, digital electronics, automatic control and analysis of electrical power systems.

I worked in CORPOELEC (Venezuelan national electrical company) conducting electrical studies such as load flow, short-circuit, transient stability and study of overvoltages. I developed a dynamic model of the electrical network for studies of overvoltages in submarine cables during opening and closing operations I have worked in CASIMA (national steel complex) as an instrumentalist, performing maintenance supervision tasks of sensors and electrical and electronic equipment

During this last year I decided to dedicate myself to learning about modern technologies such as Machine learning and Deep learning using the Python programming language. I have completed the following Udemy courses :“Complete Machine Learning and Data Science: Zero to Mastery” (43 hours), “Curso completo de Machine Learning: Data Science en Python”(49 hours), with which ones I learned to develop several types of predictive models for regression problems, classification (binary and multiple), both for structured and unstructured data.

I am particularly interested in models based on neural networks and decision trees. I also learned about data manipulation and processing using Python libraries such as Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlit, TensorFlow, Scikit-learn. You can see all my projects by visiting my repository at githud. I am excited to be able to integrate machine learning knowledge with electrical engineering in order to create intelligent and more efficient electrical systems in both generation, transmission and distribution.
